About Us

Welcome to the Sri Lanka Bus Timetable website! We are a dedicated team of five passionate individuals—Prabath Fernando, Sahan Dassana, Gayanka Dharmasena, and Nethmi Dhananjana—committed to making public transport more accessible and convenient for everyone. Our mission is to provide a reliable, user-friendly platform that connects you with accurate and up-to-date information on bus schedules across Sri Lanka. Whether you're a daily commuter, a traveler exploring the island, or simply someone looking to plan your journey, our website is here to help. We believe that better information leads to better journeys, and our goal is to empower you with the tools to navigate Sri Lanka's bus network efficiently. Thank You!
